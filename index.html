About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Epidemiology and Health Statistics at Xi'an Jiaotong University. From 2023 to 2025, I was a visiting Ph.D. student at Monash University in Melbourne, Australia.

My research interests lie at the intersection of Infectious Disease Modelling, Health Economics, and Clinical Trials. My PhD project focuses on using mathematical models and statistical methods to evaluate the impact and cost-effectiveness of health interventions, particularly for sexually transmitted infections (STIs) like Syphilis and Gonorrhea.
